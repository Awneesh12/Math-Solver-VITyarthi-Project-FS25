MATH SOLVER SYSTEM

--- Problem Statement ---

We as a human often face difficulty in performing complex or repetitive calculations manually. Therefore, we may need different software that can be used for different tasks.

To solve this, a centralized program is required that performs a wide range of tasks, quickly and accurately, with easy-to-use menu, that doesnâ€™t confuse us or make us memorize complex formulae.

--- Scope of the Project ---

This program supports 9 types of Math Problems -
- Basic Calculation (Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, and Exponential)
- Quadratic Equation Solver
- Two Linear Equation with Two Variables Solver
- LCM and HCF Calculation
- Prime Number Verifier and Prime Factor Calculator
- Statistical Calculations (Mean, Median, Mode)
- Area and Perimeter Calculator (Square, Rectangle, Circle, Triangle, Parallelogram)
- Factorial Calculator
- Matrix Operations (Addition, Multiplication, and Inverse)

--- Target Users ---

- Students: For quickly verifying solutions and understanding concepts.
- Engineers an Technical Professionals: For rapid on-the-spot calculations during designing, analysis or testing.
- Everyday Users: Anyone needing a quick and accurate solution to common mathematical problems.

--- High-Level Features ---

1. Menu-Driven Interface -
	Simple numeric inputs for navigation of all features.
2. Multiple Problem Support -
	Supporting wide variety of Math Problems like Algebra, Statistics, Equations, and more.
3. Error Handling -
	Includes checks for Mathematical Impossibilities like Dividing by Zero, finding inverse of a Singular Matrix, etc.
